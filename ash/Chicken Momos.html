    • Chicken Momos

    Serves 6 people

    Peperation Time=10min.

    Total Time=40min.

    Calories=400

    Ingredients

    Dough

    • 1 cup plain flour (maida)
    • 2 tbsp oil , 1\2 tsb salt

    Filling (Mix Together)

    • 200gm chicken mince-wash and squeeze through a muslin cloth
    • 1 onion-finely chopped
    • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
    • 2 green chillies-finely chopped
    • 2 tbsp finely chopped coriander
    • 1 tbsp salt and 1\2 tbsp pepper powder
    • 1 tbsp vineger
    • 1\2 tbsp soya sauce

    Methods

      Step 1:Chop The Vegetables And Wash Chicken

  • Mix maida,salt and oil knead with enough hot water to make a dough of rolling consistency.Keep aside covered for 30 minutes.
    Then take a clean chopping board,chop the vegetables separately and keep them aside


      • Step 2:Boil The Chicken And Then Shred Well

  • Then take a pan or a pressure cooker and boil the chicken,with a pinch of salt and pepper.If you want to make it aromatic,you can add a small teaspoon of ginger and chilli paste,you can add a small teaspoon of the garlic paste as well.Once done,carefully shred the boiled chicken into fine pieces.Keep it aside.

    • >

      Step 3:Prepare The Dough And Filling For Chicken Momos

  • Roll out the dough thinly.Cut into circles with the rim of a tea cup .Place a circle on your left hand, slightly stretch the edges and place about a heaped tbsp of filling.
    With your right thumb and forefinger pleat dough altogether over the center,add shredded chicken followed by chopped vegetables,mix well and combine everything together.then add soy sauce to the chicken mix and stir well.Now take the prepared dough and roll out small balls out forming a pin-wheel design.


      • Step 4:Flatten The Dough Ball And Fill It With Chicken Stuffing ,And Steam for 20 Minute

  • Using a rolling pin,flatten the balls in a square shape and add the chicken and veggies stuffing at the center. Bring the edges close and secure them to make a moms.Repeat this step with the remaninig balls.Transfer the prepared moms in a steamer and steam for 20 minutes or until they turn soft and tender.To make it more flavoursome,add hot with your choice of chutney or dip until done.


      • Step 5:Now Last

  • Serve!
